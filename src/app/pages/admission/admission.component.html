Admission Process

At Pinnaclo Educare, we follow a structured and merit-based admission process to ensure that students receive the best possible academic guidance. Admission to our courses is granted through a Scholarship cum Admission Test (P-SAT or PTQE), conducted annually. This test evaluates a student’s aptitude, foundational knowledge, and subject proficiency, helping us assess their readiness for our programs.

Syllabus

  • Mental Aptitude – Analytical and logical reasoning skills.
  • Previous Class Mathematics & Science – Conceptual understanding and application of key topics.

Exam Pattern

  • Format: Multiple-choice questions (MCQs) with a single correct answer.
  • Objective: To assess conceptual clarity, problem-solving skills, and analytical abilities.

Parents’ Seminar

We believe in building a strong partnership between students, parents, and educators. During the admission test, we conduct an exclusive seminar for parents, where we:

  • Provide insights into the latest examination patterns and trends.
  • Explain our teaching methodologies and academic approach.
  • Introduce our experienced faculty members and their expertise.

This session helps parents gain a clear understanding of their child’s educational journey and future opportunities.

Interview & Counselling Session

Following the test, selected students are invited for a personalized interaction with our faculty. This one-on-one session serves two purposes:

  • Career Guidance: Our experts help students explore the best career paths based on their strengths and aspirations.
  • Academic Counselling: We provide tailored advice on how to approach upcoming studies effectively, ensuring a smooth learning experience.

This session ensures that every student receives the right guidance and motivation to excel in their academic journey.
